FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical engagement take?

Web and network penetration tests typically take 1-2 weeks. Red team engagements range from 4 weeks to several months depending on objectives and scope. Adversary simulations are usually 2-4 weeks.

Do you work with small companies or only enterprise?

We work with organizations of all sizes. While we specialize in complex enterprise environments, we also help startups and small businesses with focused, high-impact assessments.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep experience in financial services, healthcare, technology, and critical infrastructure. However, our methodology adapts to any industry with specific threat models.

Do you provide remediation support?

Yes. Our reports include detailed remediation guidance. We also offer verification testing to confirm fixes are effective and retesting at reduced rates for existing clients.
