Our Story

Why Lynx Exists

Lynx Security was founded by offensive security practitioners who were frustrated with checkbox security audits and automated scan reports that missed critical vulnerabilities.

We set out to build something different: a boutique offensive security firm that delivers the depth, precision, and actionable intelligence that we'd want as clients ourselves.

Today, we serve enterprise clients across finance, healthcare, technology, and critical infrastructure, while running a free academy to train the next generation of security professionals.

Values

How We Operate

01

Precision Over Volume

We take on fewer engagements to ensure each one receives the attention and expertise it deserves. Quality assessments require time and focus.

02

Impact Over Noise

We focus on vulnerabilities that matter. A single critical vulnerability with business impact is worth more than a hundred low-severity findings.

03

Tradecraft Over Theory

Our team consists of active operators, not just certified consultants. We teach and execute based on real-world experience, not textbook knowledge.

04

Community Over Competition

We open source tools, run a free academy, and share research. Security improves when knowledge flows freely, not when it's locked behind paywalls.
